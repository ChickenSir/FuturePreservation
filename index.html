Outline

Waste pollution is damaging the environment we live in today. As more and more plastic pollutes the ocean, marine life are forced to live in harsh conditions that threaten their existence.

If this threat is left unattended, it can be damaging to our oceans and ecosystems that thrive on our planet. Together we can make a difference and prevent further damages as a community.

There is still time to stop the affects of waste pollution on our environment. Together as a community we can work together and prevent further damage. We will also be providing a challenge for University Students with biology degrees. You can find more information on how you can make a difference on our support page.
